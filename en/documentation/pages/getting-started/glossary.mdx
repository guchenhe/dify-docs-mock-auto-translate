# Glossary

Key terms and definitions for Dify.

## A

**Agent**: An autonomous AI component that can make decisions and take actions based on its instructions and available tools.

**API Key**: A unique identifier used to authenticate requests to Dify's API.

## D

**Dify**: An open-source platform for building AI workflows and applications.

## L

**LLM (Large Language Model)**: A type of AI model trained on vast amounts of text data, capable of understanding and generating human-like text.

## N

**Node**: A single step or component in a Dify workflow that performs a specific function.

## W

**Workflow**: A sequence of connected nodes that define how data flows and is processed in your AI application.

**WebApp**: A web-based interface for your Dify application that users can access through their browser.
