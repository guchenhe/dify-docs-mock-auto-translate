# Pricing & Plans

Understanding Dify pricing options.

## Free Tier

Perfect for getting started:
- Up to 100 workflow runs per month
- Basic AI models included
- Community support
- Self-hosted option available

## Pro Plan

For growing teams:
- Unlimited workflow runs
- Advanced AI models
- Priority support
- Custom integrations
- Team collaboration features

## Enterprise

Custom solutions for large organizations:
- Dedicated infrastructure
- SLA guarantees
- Advanced security features
- Custom model training
- On-premises deployment

## Self-Hosted

Deploy Dify on your own infrastructure at no cost. Perfect for organizations that need full data control.
