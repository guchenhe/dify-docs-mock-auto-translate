Dify is an open-source agentic workflow platform that lets you build AI apps by connecting your models, data, and tools with intuitive drag-and-drop workflows instead of complex coding. Whether you're building chatbots, agents, or complex AI workflows, Dify provides the tools you need to create powerful applications quickly and efficiently.
