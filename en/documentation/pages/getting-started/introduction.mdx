Dify is an open-source platform for building agentic workflows. It lets you define processes visually, connect your existing tools and data sources, and deploy AI applications that solve real problems.

## Why Choose Dify?

**Visual Workflow Builder**: Design complex AI workflows with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface. No coding required for basic use cases.

**Enterprise-Ready**: Built for scale with security, compliance, and deployment options that meet enterprise requirements.

**Extensible Platform**: Easily integrate with your existing tools, databases, and APIs through our comprehensive plugin system.
